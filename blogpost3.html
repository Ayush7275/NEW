Polycystic Ovarian Disease (PCOD) and Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) are common endocrine disorders affecting millions of women worldwide. Characterized by irregular menstrual cycles, ovarian cysts, and hormonal imbalances, these conditions can lead to various health complications such as infertility, obesity, diabetes, and cardiovascular issues. While conventional treatments focus on medication and lifestyle changes, yoga has emerged as a complementary therapy offering significant benefits for managing PCOD and PCOS. This essay explores how yoga can aid in managing these conditions, highlighting its physical, mental, and emotional benefits.
