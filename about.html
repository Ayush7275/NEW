About Avyaanam Yoga

At Avyaanam Yoga, we are dedicated to empowering individuals on their journey to holistic health and well-being. Founded in 2021 by Shubhi Dubey, a passionate advocate for yoga and its transformative benefits, Avyaanam Yoga offers a diverse range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

About the Co-founder:

Shubhi Dubey is a Certified Yoga Instructor accredited by the Yoga Certification Board of the Ayush Ministry in India. She has also completed RYS200 Yoga courses with the Yoga Alliance Group, solidifying her expertise and dedication to the practice of yoga.

Shubhi's journey with yoga began when she faced a serious abdominal problem, prompting medical advice for surgery. Determined to explore alternative healing methods, she turned to yoga and experienced remarkable recovery. This profound personal experience ignited her passion for yoga and inspired her to co-found Avyaanam Yoga with a mission to spread awareness and accessibility to yoga worldwide.

As an approved and active member of the Fit India movement, Shubhi is committed to promoting physical activity and holistic well-being on a national scale. Her vision for Avyaanam Yoga is rooted in compassion, inclusivity, and the belief that yoga has the power to transform lives. Join us at Avyaanam Yoga and embark on a journey to health, vitality, and inner peace.
