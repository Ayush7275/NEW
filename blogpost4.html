Yoga for Thyroid and Diabetes Management:

A Holistic Approach to Health

The thyroid gland, located in the neck, produces hormones that regulate metabolism. Thyroid disorders, such as hypothyroidism (underactive thyroid) and hyperthyroidism (overactive thyroid), disrupt this balance, leading to a range of symptoms including weight fluctuations, fatigue, and mood changes. Diabetes, characterized by high blood sugar levels, results from either insufficient insulin production (Type 1 diabetes) or insulin resistance (Type 2 diabetes). Both conditions require careful management to prevent complications like cardiovascular disease, neuropathy, and vision problems.
